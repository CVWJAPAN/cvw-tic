How to Start a Grief Care Support Program

Practical steps for implementing grief care in your community

1. Define Your Purpose

Clarify who you want to help — children, parents, the elderly, or general bereaved families.

2. Create Safe Spaces

  • Set up in-person or online support group sessions.
  • Ensure privacy, emotional safety, and trained facilitators.

3. Provide Resources

  • Reading materials, videos, grief journals
  • Links to professional counselors or local services

4. Offer Communication Channels

Use email forms, LINE support, or anonymous message boxes for people to reach out anytime.

5. Collaborate

Work with schools, hospitals, churches, and NPOs to expand your reach and trust.
